27th January 2018

St Andrews, Scotland

Accommodation

We appreciate that so many of our guests are travelling long distances to join us for our wedding so we have chosen a venue, Cambo Estate, which will allow us to offer on-site accommodation for nearly all of our guests.

Cambo is a beautiful old country estate located 10 minutes outside of St Andrews. The house is divided into wings and apartments, which will allow us to keep groups of friends and families together and hopefully make the weekend more special for everyone.

In the RSVP section, you will be provided with the option to stay in the house. The house accommodation includes Friday, Saturday and Sunday nights and costs approximately £75 total for all three nights per person. We would love for you to stay with us for the weekend so please do let us know when you RSVP if you are interested in this option and we will try to accommodate as many people as possible. Once we have all of the RSVPs, we will contact you with further information regarding your stay.

Note

Should you choose to stay at Cambo, we have arranged corkage for the full weekend. We will be providing beer, wine and soft drinks during the receptions but if you would like additional drinks there is an honesty bar in the Smoking Lounge or feel free to bring your favourite from duty free.

Transport

Plane


The closest airport to St Andrews is Edinburgh airport (EDI). Please continue your journey by one of the methods below.

Car Rental

As there is much to explore around St Andrews and Cambo, we would recommend hiring a car. The cost for a weekend hire can be as low as £30 if you book in advance.

Train

From London the Virgin East Coast trainline will take you from Kings Cross to Edinburgh Waverly. From there you can take a ScotRail train to Leuchars. The total journey time is about 6 hours. Leuchars is ten minutes outside of St Andrews by bus (99) or taxi.

From the airport Get one of the many buses traveling from the airport into Edinburgh city centre and get a train from Waverley or Haymarket Station to Leuchars. Leuchars is ten minutes outside of St Andrews by bus (99) or taxi.

Bus

From the airport get the 747 bus to Ferrytoll. From there, get the X24 or X59 bus to St Andrews and a taxi to Cambo.

Taxis

If required you will be able to use taxis but please ensure you call in plenty of time.

Saturday 27th January

Breakfast

Cambo is largely self-catered but should you like a full English breakfast to line your stomach, you will be able to book this closer to the time for a small cost.

Ceremony

The ceremony starts at 12pm on Saturday and will be held at St Salvator's Chapel in the university quad. We'd ask that you please arrive at 11.30am to ensure that you have time to find your seat and have a look around the iconic building. The journey from Cambo to the chapel is 20 minutes and there is a range of free on street parking available to the east of the town on North Street and South Street as well as in Greyfriars carpark. The rest of the town is pay and display.

Carriages are at midnight but you are welcome to continue the party in your apartments.

Other

Power

If you are travelling from outside of the UK, you will need adaptors for your plugs.

Money

The currency in the UK is pound sterling (GBP). You can exchange money through your local bank or in the airport for a fee.

Gift Registry

Your presence at our wedding is the best gift we could ask for so please do not feel the need to bring something extra. However, if you would like to give a gift or card, we have a honeymoon registry or there will be a table at the reception.

UK Border Control

If you are travelling from the US to the UK, you do not need a visa. However, you will need to fill out a landing card. The questions are straight-forward but please keep in mind that you will need to provide the address of where you are staying in Scotland.

Phones

The country code for the UK is +44. Local sim cards can be purchased at the airport. WiFi is available at Cambo and Edinburgh Airport.

Supermarkets

The closest supermarket is the Co-Op in Crail.

There are a number of supermarkets in St Andrews including a large Morrisons, Tesco Metro and Sainsburys Local
